What's About

1280 is freely based on Jims Morrison 1969 novel pop.1280. This game brings us to rural fascist Spain in 1950 where we incarnate Salva, a Guardia Civil agent set to uncover the mistery of how a little village of only a thousand people was deserted in the span of a day.
